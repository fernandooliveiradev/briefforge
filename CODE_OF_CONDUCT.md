# Code of Conduct

BriefForge should be a practical, respectful space for building useful software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Give clear context in issues and pull requests.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information or secrets.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participation when needed to keep the project healthy.
